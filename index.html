About Us

Aqua Clean Facility Services Company is a prominent cleaning company, initially founded in Hannover Germany. The company expanded to the GCC, to operate as PuraClean in Dubai, UAE and Aqua Clean in Doha, Qatar.

Service Quality is delivered through our team of highly trained personnel, who are committed to meeting the clientele’s needs and requirements; our ultimate aim remains to establish a secure and friendly cleaning experience.

SERVICES IN DEPTH

01

Façade Cleaning Services

Our Façade Cleaning Services stand as an effective and efficient solution to your needs. In providing excellence, we utilize 99.9% Pure Water systems to provide stain free glass cleaning solutions. We increase weather resistance while lowering possibilities of corrosion, bringing forth a positive image while increasing the value of your property

a. Rope Access - IRATA Qualified Team
The advantage of using rope access methods mainly lies in the safety and speed with which workers can get to or from difficult locations and then carry out their work, often with minimal impact on other operations and the nearby area. Another major benefit is that the combination of the total man-hours and the level of risk for a particular task (man-at-risk hours) is often reduced when compared with other means of access and their associated risks and costs

We always follow IRATA standards and make in-depth risk assessments before we start. To ensure extra safety, all our rope access teams compromise of Rope access technicians and one supervisor to plan and oversee the operation.

b. Pole Access with 25 meters high – utilizing Aqua Clean’s Pure Water Pole
The advantage of the Pole Access System is that it doesn’t limit window cleaners to just cleaning windows. It can be used to clean virtually anything, from roofs to commercial signage and cladding. Most importantly, it reduces the risk of injury and damage to property, while only using pure water to clean windows.

02

General Cleaning & Deep Cleaning

Our personnel are highly trained and equipped to handle all types of cleaning. We can offer a wide range of commercial cleaning services including daily services. Cleaning personnel will visually check and identify all areas for any type of debris, paper or spots and stains. Routine checks are conducted to keep all areas sanitized. Our team of cleaners will clean all offices, internal areas, all office furniture and other equipment will be wiped and cleaned carefully. Carpets will be dusted and vacuumed, garbage bins emptied periodically, and glass doors and windows cleaned on a regular basis. Aqua Clean adopts a “Color Coding System”, to avoid cross-contamination.

We provide these services to various facilities, such as:

  • Factories
  • Nurseries
  • Hospitals
  • Construction
  • Universities
  • Hotels
  • Clinics
  • Restaurants
  • Ministries
